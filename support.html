Frequently Asked Questions

How can I cancel my booking?

You can cancel your booking by visiting the "My Bookings" section, selecting your ticket, and clicking the "Cancel" button. Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days.

What should I do if my train is delayed?

Check the "PNR Status" or "Train Schedule" section for real-time updates. If you need further assistance, contact our support team at 8830707410.

Can I change my travel date after booking?

Yes, you can modify your travel date in the "My Bookings" section, subject to availability and applicable charges. Please do this at least 24 hours before departure.

How do I get a refund for a cancelled train?

If a train is cancelled by TicketXpress, a full refund will be automatically processed to your original payment method within 3-5 business days.
